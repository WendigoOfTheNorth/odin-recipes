Buttered Noodles

Description

This simple pasta dish is an easy and affordable option for those struggling finacially.

It is a staple in our home.

Ingredients

  • Rotini (or your own favorite noode)
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil
  2. Add Pasta and cook for 7 minutes
  3. Strain Noodles
  4. Add to bowl with 1 tablespoon of butter, season with salt, pepper, and parmesan.
